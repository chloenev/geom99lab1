Stops Along the Way

Day Location Story
1 Stouffville → Sault Ste. Marie Visited the Big Nickel in Sudbury. Finished our first day of driving in Sault Ste. Marie.
2 Sault Ste. Marie → Thunder Bay Stopped in at Old Woman Bay in Lake Superior Provincial Park. Said hi to Winnie the Pooh in White River. Saw some deer and got some rest in Thunder Bay.
3 Thunder Bay → Winnipeg Stood at the Longitudal Centre of Canada In Manitoba!
4 Winnipeg → Swift Current Not a whole lot to report on this stretch. Mainly just hot and flat. Did I mention the car had no AC?
5 Swift Current → Canmore Of course we had to make a pit stop to see the Hoo Doos in Drumheller. Very cool!
6 Canmore → Our new home! My favourite part of the drive - through the mountains.
